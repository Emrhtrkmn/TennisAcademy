ACADEMY

The MKTA is relaunching the junior support concept "the MKTA way". As part of this, three to four gatherings are organized per year for all Young Talents & competition students. The aim is to provide more intensive support for “particularly motivated kids” from the Orange level. Two tennis training sessions per week are required. You can find more information in the graphic «the MKTA way». An important part of this «Young Talents Family» is meeting like-minded people. Maybe you will find a "new Gpädli" to "play freely" in the future.
